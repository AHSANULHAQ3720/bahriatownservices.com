  Submission of Application

 

  1. Fill in the prescribed application form (CAPITAL LETTERS) and submit it in your Bahria customer services office  nearest to your home along with the following attested CNIC Copy.
  2. Proof of the title of property to ascertain size of plot.
  3. Gas bill of the neighbor nearest to your home.
  4. Get a proper receipt in acknowledgment of your application. This receipt will be used for any subsequent reference or query regarding your application.
